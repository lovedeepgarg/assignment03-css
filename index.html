About Chocolate Caramel Brownie Ice Cream

Chocolate caramel brownie ice cream is a decadent treat combining rich chocolate, gooey caramel, and chewy brownie pieces. It's the perfect dessert for any chocolate lover, offering a blend of textures and flavors that's simply irresistible.

Interesting Facts

  • Chocolate contains antioxidants that can benefit heart health.
  • Caramel is made by heating sugar, which gives it a unique flavor and texture.
  • Brownies add a chewy texture, enhancing the overall ice cream experience.

Recipe

  1. Prepare brownie batter and bake in a square pan. Once cooled, cut into small pieces.
  2. In a medium saucepan, combine the sugar, heavy cream, and whole milk. Cook over medium heat until the sugar has dissolved.
  3. Remove from heat and stir in the chocolate and vanilla extract until the chocolate is melted.
  4. Pour the mixture into an ice cream maker and churn according to the manufacturer's instructions. During the last few minutes, add the caramel sauce and brownie pieces.
  5. Transfer the ice cream to a container and freeze for at least 2 hours before serving.
